§ 01 Findings

Two things the dashboard won't say.

01 LedeMedium

Numbers arrive without a narrator.

The page opens with three stat cards — AUM, NAV change, Flows — and a bar chart. A reader has to assemble their own sentence from the figures. The page should hand them one.

Lede
One-paragraph editorial summary in serif italic: "A quiet quarter — AUM edged up, flows held, and the portfolio's two long-dated holdings carried the month."
02 ChartMedium

The bar chart is chart junk.

The chart shows monthly NAV as 12 indigo bars. No axis labels, no annotations, no point of view — just shape. A chart that doesn't make a point isn't worth the space.

Annotate or remove
If kept, add a single annotation: the month that moved. Otherwise drop it and put the words there.
