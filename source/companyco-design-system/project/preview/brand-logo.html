The mark is two open arcs on the same axis — an outer holding an inner, a company holding its record. Nothing more. It works at the scale of a seal and the scale of a favicon, and is made to be trusted at a glance.

§ 01 Construction

Two concentric arcs, offset to the right. No terminals, no serifs, no ring — only one quiet dot of registration.
01.aGeometry
Concentric radii of 62u and 22u on a shared axis. Stroke ends are round; the line ends where the arc ends.
01.bMeaning
The outer C holds the company. The inner C holds the record. They share an axis — never a line.
01.cClear space
Minimum margin x = the stroke width. Nothing crosses the square; nothing sits inside the outer arc except the inner.

§ 05 Rules

Trust is the absence of surprise. These are the non-negotiables.
✓   Do
  • Keep both arcs visible at ≥ 24 px. Below that, outer only.
  • Set the wordmark in Playfair Display, italicise Co, amber-only.
  • Leave one stroke-width of clear space on every side.
  • Let amber earn its place — one soft moment per surface.
×   Don't
  • Don't recolour into a gradient, outline, or metallic finish.
  • Don't add a seal ring, serifs, or drop-shadow.
  • Don't pair the mark with blue, emojis, or secondary logos.
  • Don't letterspace, italicise or bold the wordmark further.
