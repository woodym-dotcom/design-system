BODY

Lede · 14.5

CompanyCo is the system of record for how a regulated business performs against its own controls. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, shared drives and heroic Monday-morning emails with a single, auditable ledger.

The voice is terse, domain-literal, second person, present tense. Sentence case, always. No marketing fluff inside the product. The brand speaks in the same register the regulator does.
